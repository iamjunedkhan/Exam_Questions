Write example of Commercial Mobile Operating Systems and also explain them?

A commercial mobile operating system (OS) is an operating system that is developed and marketed by a commercial company for use in mobile devices such as smartphones, tablets, and other portable devices.

Examples of commercial mobile operating systems include Apple's iOS, Google's Android, and Microsoft's Windows Mobile. These operating systems are designed to run on a variety of mobile devices and provide a range of features and functionalities, including support for mobile apps, touch-based user interfaces, and connectivity to mobile networks.

Commercial mobile operating systems are typically licensed to device manufacturers and mobile network operators, who can then customize and distribute devices running the operating system to their customers. They are often designed to work seamlessly with other commercial products and services, such as app stores, cloud services, and other mobile-related offerings.

Overall, commercial mobile operating systems have become an integral part of modern mobile devices, providing the foundation for the functionality and user experience of smartphones, tablets, and other mobile devices.
